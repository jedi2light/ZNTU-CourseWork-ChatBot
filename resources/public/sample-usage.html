Sample Usage

Medicines Order

  1. First you have to start a dialog with one of the following phrases:
__Hello__!
Let`s __start__!
I __want__ to __order__ some medicines.
  1. Next you have to send a medicine in this format:
{medicine} {amount} (i.e.): Medicine Name 10
  1. Next you have to confirm your enter with one of the following keywords:
Yes, Yep, Yeah, Sure
  1. When you finish, you have to send one of the next phrases:
I have __done__.
__Enough__.
__Finished__.
  1. Bot will send you a formatted string with a result
  2. Next you have to thank your bot for help
__Thank__ you!

Guess Your Diasease

  1. First you have to start a dialog with one of the following phrases:
__Hello__!
Let`s __start__!
Got __sick__...
  1. Next you have to send a symptom: Please take a look at db-bootstrap-data/009-mixed-diaseases-symptoms.sql for supported symptoms
  2. When you finish, you have to send one of the next phrases:
I have __done__.
__Enough__.
__Finished__.
  1. Bot will send you a formatted string with a result
  2. Next you have to thank your bot for help
__Thank__ you!
